Hello, I'm Ryan Dawson.
I am an artist and designer, outdoorsman, life-long learner, and fashionista (as you can tell, from my profile image). I’ve been described as chaotic good, an enigma, and a cool dude. I earned my bachelors degree in studio arts from Monmouth College, where I focused on Ceramics, Ceramic Engineering, and Photography. My background in art continues to inspire my everyday life, leading me to User Experience and User Interface Design, where I live in the realm of wireframe and aesthetic design.
My Skills

Low-Fidelity Wireframing

Using paper and pencil, I am able to quickly design a website or app’s wireframe based on the data collected from the UX team’s user interviews

Iterating

I am able to quickly produce iterations based upon user feedback and stakeholder input.

High-Fidelity Prototyping

Once the product is to a point of satisfaction in the low-fidelity design, I am able to transition the product into higher fidelity prototypes.
